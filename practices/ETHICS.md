# Ethics & Best Practices

Before publishing a reflection, please confirm:

- [ ] The note contains no confidential, embargoed, or proprietary material.  
- [ ] Opinions are clearly distinguished from factual statements.  
- [ ] Collaborative reflections are shared with consent of co-authors.  
- [ ] References and quotations are properly attributed.  
- [ ] Tone remains constructive and inclusive.

Corrections are made by appending a new reflection tagged `erratum`.

This repository contains no personal data and no human-subject information.
